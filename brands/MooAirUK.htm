MooAir UK

MooAir UK manages MooAir's customer service operations in Europe. It also operates various entertainment and flight services brands.

MooAir UK is MooAir's sister company, owned by MooAir Holdings LTD. MooAir UK is a vital part of MooAir's services and customer management operations in Europe. It also operates select brands that enable our customers to go further, and do more, while they're in the the land of the royals.

While the MooAir UK brand may no longer be flying (apart from select MooAir Australia operated flights), its fleet is currently in use elsewhere. Contact MooAir Customer Support for more info about how to transfer from a MooAir Australia flight into our European operation and partners.
