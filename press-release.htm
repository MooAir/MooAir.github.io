Your Attention, Please.

MooAir today announces the re-launch of MooAir UK after rebranding its subsidy Jet2.com.

Press Release

Today, MooAir UK rebrands its subsidy, Jet2.com, to become MooAir UK, after its parent company. Under the new MooAir UK brand, the airline will bring even better service to more destinations, matching, and exceeding, the service that customers have come to expect from MooAir. Jet2’s fleet will be rolled over to MooAir UK, with all of its pending leases being fulfilled by the company. MooAir UK will also bring new in-flight entertainment and services aboard our domestic - and soon to be international - routes. Jet2’s customers can expect an even better service that appeals to more people, including business travellers. MooAir UK cannot wait to welcome you aboard.

MooAir makes flying sexy, now in the UK. Stay fly, ‘cause we’re gonna live it on up in the sky.
