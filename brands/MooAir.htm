MooAir

MooAir makes flying sexy. Stay Fly, 'cause we're gonna live it on up in the sky.

MooAir is our largest brand, flying international and domestic routes to, from, and around Australia. Based in Sydney, MooAir operates over 40 routes with over 30 aircraft in its fleet. It defined and continues to re-define what to expect from an airline.

MooAir flies to all major cities in Australia, as well as select international routes to destinations such as South Korea, Indonesia, and the United States.
